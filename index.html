A bit about me

I'm a 25-year-old from China, currently based in Hong Kong. I studied Legal Studies at UC Berkeley, lived in a community of students from 77 countries, worked as a barista, and fell in love with the working world.

Now I work at Jane Street in Hong Kong, where I'm building toward a career in strategy and product. Outside of work, I write, run half marathons, explore jazz bars, and think about what it means to grow up between two cultures.
