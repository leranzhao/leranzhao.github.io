I've been meaning to build a personal website for a while now. Not because I need one — most of what I do lives in emails, conversations, and internal docs. But because I wanted a place that's mine. A corner of the internet where I can think out loud.

A lot has happened in the last two years. I graduated from Berkeley, moved to Hong Kong, started a new career, and kept writing — mostly in private, mostly in Chinese, mostly for no audience. This site is an experiment in changing that last part.

I'll write about work, identity, living between cultures, and whatever else I'm thinking about. Some posts will be in English, some in Chinese. No schedule, no pressure.

If you're here, welcome. Pull up a chair.

— Leran
